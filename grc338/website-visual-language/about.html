About Glow & Co.

Welcome to Glow & Co., where we ignite your senses with our handcrafted candles in the heart of Malibu, California. As artisans of ambiance, we specialize in creating cool and customizable candles that elevate any space. From the sun-kissed shores of Malibu to the cozy corners of your home, our unique blends and personalized options ensure that every candle illuminates your story. Join us in illuminating moments, creating memories, and spreading the glow with Glow & Co..

At Glow & Co., our mission is to infuse every moment with warmth, light, and personal touch. We strive to craft candles that not only enhance the ambiance of any space but also reflect the unique style and personality of our customers. Our goal is to continuously innovate and create cool, customizable candles that inspire creativity and connection, bringing joy and comfort to homes and hearts worldwide. We are dedicated to sustainability, using eco-friendly materials and processes to minimize our environmental footprint while spreading the glow of positivity and community in every candle we create.

Glow & Co. was born from a passion for crafting unique and personalized candles that illuminate moments and evoke memories. Founded in the picturesque coastal town of Malibu, California, our journey into the candle business began with a desire to bring warmth, light, and a personal touch to every space. Inspired by the beauty of our surroundings and the vibrant energy of our community, we set out to create candles that capture the essence of coastal living while reflecting the diverse tastes and preferences of our customers.
