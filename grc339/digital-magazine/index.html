The Concrete Waves of Venice Beach

Discover the rich history of skateboarding in Venice Beach, where the culture was born and continues to thrive. Explore the legendary spots, iconic skaters, and the evolution of the skate scene in this unique coastal community.

Full Send

Every skater has a story, and every story is a journey. Unfortunately, most of these journeys lead to some injuries. The skater mindset has always reflected the concept of "full send". No cares or worries; a just do it kind of mentality. With great risk comes great reward. Keep on sending!

Flippin Fashion

Skate fashion has evolved dramatically over the years. Skaters express their individuality through their distinctive fashion choices. Today's skatewear includes everything from classic sneakers and graphic tees to modern streetwear trends. A classic skater fit would be composed of some baggy jeans, cool kicks, and a fun, comfortable top or hoodie.

Pick Your Park

Different skate parks offer unique features and challenges. When picking your park, find its standout attributes such as interesting bowls, creative rails, and the overall atmosphere. Feel out the vibe and see if its the park for you!

What's the Board for you?

Selecting the right skateboard can make a big difference in your riding experience. You can choose from Penny boards, Longboards, Cruisers, and classic boards. Each ride a different way and have a different style. Check out your local skate shop and test some oout. You'll know your board when you ride it!

Boarding Beats

Music is a vital part of many skate sessions, providing energy and rhythm. It can be satisfying to time your jumps and tricks to the music you're listening to. Since skating can be a very personal activity and sport, listening to music can be very theraputic as well. Shred recommends you make a skate playlist. Can you feel the difference.

Up and Coming

There are always fresh faces making their mark in the skateboarding world. These rising stars are pushing boundaries with innovative tricks and fresh styles. Watch out for their future achievements!

Evolution of Skateboarding

Skateboarding has evolved from simple wooden boards to high-tech designs and complex tricks. Some of the easiest tricks to accomplish now were some of the cool new moves when skating got off the ground. It's exciting to see the sport expand and grow!

Boarding Abroad

Skateboarding is a global phenomenon, with skaters making their mark in diverse international locations. Some of the Venice Beachs around the world include Barcelona's MACBA, Southbank in London, and Love Park in Philadelphia

The Youth

Skateboarding has a significant impact on young people, fostering creativity and building communities. These young skaters are quickly making their mark with impressive skills and fresh perspectives.

Skater Girls

Girls are breaking barriers and redefining skateboarding with their unique style and incredible skills. Their presence and impact on the sport highlight the growing diversity and talent within the skating community.

Skate Community

The skateboarding community is characterized by its camaraderie and support. Skaters tend to charish the friendships, shared experiences, and collective passion that bind them together!
