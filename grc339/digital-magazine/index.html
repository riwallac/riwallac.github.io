The Concrete Waves of Venice Beach

Discover the rich history of skateboarding in Venice Beach, where the culture was born and continues to thrive. Explore the legendary spots, iconic skaters, and the evolution of the skate scene in this unique coastal community.

Full Send

The skater mindset has always reflected the concept of "full send". No cares or worries; a just do it kind of mentality. With great risk comes great reward. Keep on sending!

Flippin Fashion

Skaters express their individuality through their distinctive fashion choices. A classic skater fit would be composed of some baggy jeans, cool kicks, and a fun, comfortable top or hoodie.

Pick Your Park

Different skate parks offer unique features and challenges. When picking your park, find its standout attributes such as interesting bowls, creative rails, and the overall atmosphere. Feel out the vibe and see if its the park for you!

What's the Board for you?

Selecting the right skateboard can make a big difference in your riding experience. You can choose from Penny boards, Longboards, Cruisers, and classic boards. Check out your local skate shop and test some out!

Boarding Beats

Music can provide energy and rhythm while skating. It can be satisfying to time jumps and tricks to music. Since skating can be a very personal activity and sport, listening to music can be very theraputic as well.

Up and Coming

The new faces and rising stars of the skating world are pushing boundaries with innovative tricks and fresh styles. Watch out for their future achievements!

Evolution of Skateboarding

Skateboarding has evolved from simple wooden boards to high-tech designs and complex tricks. It's exciting to see the sport expand and grow!

Boarding Abroad

Skateboarding is a global phenomenon. Some of the Venice Beachs around the world include Barcelona's MACBA, Southbank in London, and Love Park in Philadelphia

The Youth

Skateboarding has a significant impact on young people. These young skaters are quickly making their mark with impressive skills and fresh perspectives.

Skater Girls

Girls are breaking barriers and redefining skateboarding. Their presence and impact on the sport highlight the growing diversity and talent within the community.

Skate Community

The skateboarding community is characterized by its camaraderie and support. Skaters charish their friendships, shared experiences, and collective passion!
