a dive into stillwater

At Stillwater Swimwear, Sana and I, Riley, are the dynamic duo behind the scenes. Meeting at Cal Poly in San Luis Obispo, we bonded over our shared love for sewing, thrifting, and endless beach days. Coming from Seattle and LA respectively, our diverse backgrounds infuse every stitch with West Coast flair.

Our mission? Crafting bikinis that provide confidence and comfort for every body type. Picture killer cuts, vibrant prints, and a whole lot of sass. So if you're all about soaking up the sun and making a statement, join us at Stillwater Swimwear. Let's dive in and make waves together.
